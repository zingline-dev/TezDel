## Brand Context Used

﻿# TezDel Brand Profile

TezDel is a Bhubaneswar-first hyperlocal food and grocery delivery platform.

## Core Idea

Fast delivery with a local soul.

## Mission

Make every neighbourhood in Bhubaneswar self-sufficient through a trusted, fast delivery network powered by home chefs, local kiranas, restaurants, and neighbourhood captains.

## Differentiators

- Authentic Odia food through verified home chefs.
- Flat restaurant subscription instead of high commission.
- Kirana-as-dark-store model with zero real-estate capex.
- Neighbourhood captains who know the locality.
- ONDC-ready seller infrastructure.

## Launch Zones

- Patia
- Nayapalli
- Infocity corridor

## Tone

Local, warm, practical, proud of Bhubaneswar, and respectful toward small businesses.
