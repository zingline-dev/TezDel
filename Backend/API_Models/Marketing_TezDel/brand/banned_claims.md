# Banned Or Risky Claims

Avoid these unless TezDel has proof or operational approval:

- Guaranteed 20-minute delivery in every area.
- Best or cheapest delivery app in Bhubaneswar.
- Certified healthy food unless verified.
- FSSAI-approved home chef unless the individual chef is verified.
- Claims that attack competitors personally.
- Any auto-posting, mass DM, or spam instruction.

Prefer safer wording:

- Built for fast local delivery.
- Target delivery time: 20 minutes in launch zones.
- Fair flat-fee model for restaurants.
- Verified home-chef onboarding process.
