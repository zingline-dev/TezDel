# TezDel Audience Segments

## Consumers

Young professionals, students, local families, and working residents in Bhubaneswar who want fast food and grocery delivery from trusted nearby sources.

## Restaurants

Local restaurants frustrated by 22-30% marketplace commissions and looking for fairer delivery economics.

## Home Chefs

Skilled home cooks, especially women, who can prepare authentic Odia meals and want a dignified earning channel.

## Kirana Stores

Neighbourhood grocery shops that want digital orders without losing their customer relationship.

## Delivery Captains

Local part-time earners who can deliver within a familiar neighbourhood radius.
