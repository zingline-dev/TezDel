Unlike national players who simply replicate their metro model in Tier-2 cities, TezDel is designed from the ground up for the city — leveraging existing neighbourhood infrastructure, authentic Odia cuisine, and deep community trust as its primary competitive moats.The name is deliberate. 'Tez' is instantly understood in both Odia and Hindi — it means fast. 'Del' signals delivery. Together: fast delivery, with a local soul. Short, pronounceable, and memorable across every language spoken in Bhubaneswar.Core Mission StatementTo make every neighbourhood in Bhubaneswar self-sufficient through a trusted, fast delivery network — powered by home chefs, local kiranas, and community captains — not dark stores and corporate warehouses.20 minTarget Delivery Time₹0Commission to Restaurants₹999/moFlat Restaurant FeeDay 1ONDC IntegrationWhy Bhubaneswar, Why Now?Bhubaneswar is experiencing a perfect storm of conditions that make it ideal for a hyperlocal delivery startup:Rapidly growing young professional population in Infocity, Patia, and Nayapalli corridorsHigh density of IT workers and college students with disposable income and mobile-first habitsSevere underrepresentation of authentic Odia food in existing delivery platformsLocal restaurant owners who are actively frustrated by Swiggy/Zomato commission structuresTier-2 economics: breakeven at just 800 daily orders versus 1,300 in metrosFlashnow operating as the only serious local competitor — and showing the market demand is real02 — MARKET & COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPEThe BattlefieldIndia's quick-commerce market hit $5.38 billion in 2025, growing at 17% — the fastest rate globally.
